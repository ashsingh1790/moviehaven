# About Pages Strategy — Two-Page Approach

**Strategic Goal**: Create two distinct pages that serve different audiences and purposes.

---

## Page 1: About (/about)
**Focus**: The APPLICATION  
**Audience**: Users, potential users, general visitors  
**Purpose**: Explain what MovieHaven is, its features, and user value  
**Tone**: User-friendly, marketing-oriented, benefit-focused  

### Content
- What is MovieHaven
- Key features
- Why choose MovieHaven
- How it works (user journey)
- Tech stack (light version)
- Vision & roadmap
- Call-to-action (sign up / try demo)

### Phase 2 Stories: 5 stories
1. Design & build About page layout
2. Add user journey & How It Works
3. Tech stack & vision sections
4. Add CTA & demo access
5. Performance & SEO optimization

**Total effort**: ~2-3 weeks  
**Success metric**: Converts visitors to signups/demo access  

---

## Page 2: About the Project (/about/the-project)
**Focus**: DEVELOPMENT METHODOLOGY  
**Audience**: Developers, employers, technical reviewers  
**Purpose**: Showcase your skills in AI-assisted development  
**Tone**: Technical, detailed, data-driven, skill-focused  

### Content
- Your role: Orchestrating AI development
- Agent & instruction design (your expertise)
- Token optimization & LLM economics (KEY SHOWCASE)
- Quality systems & security design
- Process design for AI development
- Skills breakdown with measurements
- Comparative metrics & charts
- What sets your approach apart

### Phase 2 Stories: 6 stories
1. Showcase AI agent architecture
2. Showcase instruction design
3. Token optimization showcase (PRIMARY)
4. Quality & security system
5. Process design & workflow
6. Skills & metrics dashboard

**Total effort**: ~3-4 weeks  
**Success metric**: Demonstrates expertise to employers/developers  

---

## Audience & Use Cases

### About Page (/about)
**Who visits**:
- Casual users discovering the app
- Potential users from Google search
- Friends/family checking out your project
- Product hunters / press

**What they want**:
- Quick understanding of what the app does
- Why they should use it
- How to get started
- Cool features

**Success**: They sign up or try the demo

---

### About the Project (/about/the-project)
**Who visits**:
- Employers reviewing your portfolio
- Developers interested in your approach
- People studying AI-assisted development
- Technical decision-makers

**What they want**:
- Proof of technical expertise
- Understanding of your development process
- Evidence of code quality & security
- Knowledge of LLM economics and optimization

**Success**: They recognize your expertise and skills
